# NEXTERA ENERGY (NEE)
## Company Deep Dive | March 2026

---

## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

**NextEra Energy (NYSE: NEE)** is the largest electric power and energy infrastructure company in North America, and the world's largest generator of renewable energy from wind and solar. With a market cap of ~$186B and TTM revenue of $27.4B, the company operates through two primary segments: Florida Power & Light (FPL), a regulated utility, and NextEra Energy Resources (NEER), a wholesale energy generator.

**Key Stats (as of March 2026):**
- Market Cap: ~$186 billion
- TTM Revenue: $27.4 billion
- 2025 Adjusted EPS: $3.71 (+~10% YoY)
- Dividend Yield: ~2.8%
- Generation Capacity: 100+ GW by end of 2026

---

## BUSINESS SEGMENTS

### 1. Florida Power & Light (FPL) – Regulated Utility
**Contribution: ~60% of earnings**

FPL is the third-largest electric utility in the United States, serving ~12 million accounts across Florida. Key characteristics:

- **Regulated Returns:** Earns authorized return on equity (ROE) of ~10.5%
- **Rate Base Growth:** ~8-9% annual rate base compounding expected
- **Customer Growth:** Florida's population growth drives ~3% annual load growth
- **Infrastructure Investment:** $21.7B planned T&D investment through 2029
- **Storm Protection:** ~$14.5B in approved storm hardening investments
- **Generation Mix:** Nuclear (~40%), Natural Gas (~35%), Solar (~15%), Storage (~5%)

**Competitive Advantage:**
- Lowest customer bills among major U.S. utilities
- Highest reliability in Florida (ReliabilityOne® awards)
- Favorable regulatory framework with 4-year rate agreement approved Nov 2025

### 2. NextEra Energy Resources (NEER) – Clean Energy Development
**Contribution: ~40% of earnings**

NEER is the world's largest generator of renewable energy and a leader in battery storage:

- **Renewable Portfolio:** 30+ GW of wind and solar capacity
- **Storage Leadership:** 4+ GW of battery storage deployed/planned
- **Diversified Generation:** Natural gas, nuclear, oil alongside renewables
- **Growth Model:** Develops, constructs, and operates long-term contracted assets
- **Contracted Revenue:** 85%+ of generation under long-term PPAs

**Key Metrics:**
- Industry-leading development pipeline
- Strong contracted cash flows with creditworthy counterparties
- Strategic focus on large-load customers (data centers, AI facilities)

## INVESTMENT THESIS

### Bull Case ✓
1. **Regulated Utility Provides Stability:** FPL's regulated returns + Florida's population growth = predictable earnings
2. **Renewable Leadership:** Largest renewable portfolio with 30+ GW capacity and strong development pipeline
3. **Data Center Demand:** AI/ML boom driving massive new electricity demand; NextEra uniquely positioned to supply
4. **Storage Expansion:** 4+ GW storage deployment; critical for grid stability and renewable integration
5. **Capital Discipline:** Management committed to investment-grade balance sheet
6. **Dividend Growth:** 10% annual dividend growth through at least 2026 (may extend)

### Bear Case ✗
1. **Execution Risk:** Massive capital deployment required; execution challenges possible
2. **Regulatory Risk:** Changes in FPL rate framework could impact returns
3. **Interest Rate Sensitivity:** High debt load makes it rate-sensitive
4. **Renewable Policy Risk:** Tax credit changes could impact NEER economics
5. **Competition:** Increasing competition in renewable development space
6. **Valuation:** Trading at premium to historical averages
