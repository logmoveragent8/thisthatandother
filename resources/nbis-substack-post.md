**Nebius Group (NBIS).**

---

## What Is Nebius?

Nebius is an AI infrastructure company. In plain English: they build data centers filled with GPUs, and companies rent that compute to train and run AI models.

Think of it like... the landlord for AI companies.

The interesting story? Nebius used to be part of **Yandex** — you know, the "Google of Russia." In late 2024, Yandex spun out its cloud/infrastructure business, and it became Nebius.

Now it's a publicly traded company on NASDAQ, building AI factories across the US and Europe.

**What they do:**
- GPU cloud services (rent compute to AI companies)
- Custom-built high-density data centers
- "AI factories" — massive facilities purpose-built for AI training
- Enterprise AI infrastructure

## Why This Matters

Here's the thing about AI infrastructure: **everyone needs it.**

Every company building AI products needs compute. GPUs are scarce. And the companies with the most GPUs — and the best infrastructure to run them — have a real business.

Nebius has:
- **50,000+ GPU clusters** (custom designed)
- **Major customers** — reportedly Microsoft and Meta
- **First gigawatt AI factory** approved in Missouri (one of the largest AI data centers in the world)
- **World-class engineering** — these are former Yandex engineers who built one of the world's largest search engines

This isn't a company betting on a speculative technology. They're building the railroads for the AI economy.

---

## The Valuation Question

So here's the catch: at $22.5 billion market cap and $530 million in revenue, Nebius trades at **42x sales**.

That's expensive by traditional standards.

But here's the math:
- At 350% growth → $530M → $1B+ revenue in 2026 → 22x P/S
- At 350% growth → $2B+ revenue in 2027 → 11x P/S
- At 350% growth → $4B+ revenue in 2028 → 5.6x P/S

The valuation is aggressive but not crazy — **if the growth holds.**

Compare to D-Wave (QBTS) which trades at 400x+ sales with much smaller revenue. NBIS actually looks reasonable.

## The Bull Case

1. **Pure-play AI infrastructure** — Rare publicly traded play
2. **351% growth** — Exceptional in any market
3. **Major customer wins** — Microsoft, Meta = validation
4. **$5B capex** — Serious about scaling
5. **World-class team** — Yandex engineers are legit
6. **Gigawatt AI factories** — First-mover on scale infrastructure

---

## The Bear Case

1. **Competition** — AWS, Google, Microsoft are massive
2. **Valuation** — 42x sales prices in perfection
3. **Capital intensive** — Needs to keep spending billions
4. **Not profitable yet** — Still burning cash
5. **Geopolitical** — Russia connection could create overhang

---

## My Take

**Rating: 3.5/5 stars — Buy on Pullback**

Nebius is one of the most interesting AI infrastructure plays I've found. The growth is exceptional, the team is world-class, and the market opportunity is massive.

But at 42x revenue, there's no room for error.

**My approach:** I'd wait for a pullback to $15-18B market cap before buying. That would give me a better margin of safety.

If it drops, I'd make this a core holding in my AI portfolio.
