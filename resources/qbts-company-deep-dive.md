## 9. Investment Thesis Summary

### Bull Case (One Paragraph)

With 178% YoY growth, a dual-technology strategy (annealing + gate-model), and first-mover advantage, D-Wave is positioned to capture significant share in a market projected to reach $100B+ by 2030. As quantum computing moves from research to production, D-Wave's established customer base and proven technology could deliver outsized returns.

Despite strong growth, D-Wave trades at **over 400x sales** — a valuation that prices in perfect execution. The company burns $150M+ annually, has only 2-3 years of cash runway, and will almost certainly need to raise dilutive capital. Big Tech competitors (IBM, Google, Microsoft) have vastly more resources. Quantum computing may remain a niche technology that never achieves the scale needed to justify current valuations.
